Rockets

A space rocket is a vehicle with a very powerful jet engine designed to carry people or equipment beyond Earth and out into space. SpaceX rockets transport payloads and spacecraft into orbit. SpaceX believes rocket reusability is the key breakthrough needed to reduce the cost of access to space and enable people to live on other planets.
